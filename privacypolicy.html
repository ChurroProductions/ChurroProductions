Welcome to Churro Productions LLC! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you play our projects. By playing our projects, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

When you use our projects, we may collect the following types of information:

Information that can identify you, such as your IP address. This is used for account management and security purposes.

Device Information:

Details about the device you use to play our games, such as hardware model and operating system version.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

・ Provide and improve our games and services

・ Address technical issues and enhance user experience

・ Enforce our terms and conditions, including banning users when necessary

Third-Party Services

We may share your information with the following third-party services to improve your gaming experience:

・ Photon: Photon allows users to join lobbies and connect to voice chat. It may collect data necessary for these functions.

・ Playfab: Playfab helps us store player data, manage bans, and use IP addresses to assist with banning users who violate our terms.

Data Security

Churro Productions LLC takes reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We employ industry standard security measures to ensure your data is safe.

We do not sell or give out your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
